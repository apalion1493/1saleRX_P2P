About 1SaleRx

Revolutionizing the Way Independent Pharmacies Thrive

At 1SaleRx, we believe independent pharmacies deserve the tools and technology to compete — and win — in today’s fast-changing healthcare market.


We’re not just another platform. We’re your partner in profitability, efficiency, and smarter sourcing.

Why We Exist

The pharmaceutical supply chain is broken — inflated costs, limited visibility, and mountains of unsold inventory.


We created 1SaleRx to solve exactly that.

Our mission is simple:

To empower pharmacies to buy smarter, sell faster, and waste less.


We do that by connecting you directly to licensed wholesalers and other pharmacies — giving you the freedom to choose, compare, and control your inventory like never before.

What Makes Us Different

Lightning-fast search. Mobile-ready. No steep learning curve. Just log in and get to work.

Dispute? Resolved.

Things happen — we get it. Our platform includes built-in dispute resolution tools to quickly and fairly resolve any issues between buyers and sellers. We’re here to make sure every transaction goes smoothly.

Buy & Sell in One Place

Source from wholesalers or other pharmacies, and resell overstock — all from one intuitive platform.

Lowest Fees in the Market

We believe your profits should stay with you — that’s why we offer one of the lowest commission rates in the industry. No inflated cuts. No hidden charges. Just honest pricing.

Safe. Verified. Compliant.

From license verification to DSCSA compliance, we go the extra mile to protect every transaction. You’re not just using a marketplace — you’re joining a secure, pharmacy-only network you can trust.

Real-Time Insights

Stay one step ahead with live pricing, availability, and demand tracking — right from your dashboard. Lightning-fast search. Mobile-ready. No steep learning curve. Just log in and get to work.

Built By Experts, Inspired By Pharmacy Owners

We’re a team of healthcare innovators, tech engineers, and business strategists — but most importantly, we listen to you.


1SaleRx was built after years of speaking with independent pharmacy owners who said the same thing:

We need a better way to source and manage meds.

So we built it.

For independents. By people
who believe in them.

Let’s Grow Together

1SaleRx is rapidly expanding across the U.S., growing a trusted community of pharmacies that work smarter — together.


Our vision is to become the 1 sourcing and resale platform for independent pharmacies nationwide.


Whether you’re a single-store operator or part of a small regional group, 1SaleRx is here to help you take control of your business.

We’re more than a marketplace — we’re your strategic advantage.
